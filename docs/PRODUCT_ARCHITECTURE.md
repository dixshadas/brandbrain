# Product Architecture (reference)

This engineering scaffold implements the approved product architecture: the wedge is the
**Insight Synthesizer** (evidence → defensible, cited, contradiction-aware answer), the moat is the
**Brand Brain** (curated, governed memory built as a byproduct of use), and **trust is the
product**. See the product strategy document for personas, JTBD, the MVP thesis, and the roadmap.
The Technical Design Document (`TECHNICAL_DESIGN.md`) is the implementation of that architecture and
does not re-open its decisions.
